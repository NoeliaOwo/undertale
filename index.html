Seeking Strength

In my original composition, another woman was comforting her and lifting up her hand. That piece rang with commendable ideals but lacked the dissonance to leave an enduring impression. Now, the woman sits alone in her Gethsemane. No one has come to lift up her hand. Christ is her sole companion and source of strength. We are asked as the viewers if we will “sleep” through her struggles as the apostles did, or if we will stay by her side and “watch with [her] one hour.” (Matthew 26:40)
